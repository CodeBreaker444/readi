Welcome to the official documentation for **Readi**, an operations and compliance platform for drone teams.

## What is Readi?

Readi helps aviation and drone operations teams manage planning, missions, training, compliance, maintenance, and records in one workspace. Built to support day-to-day operations while keeping audit readiness and safety visibility across teams.

## What you can do in Readi

- Track mission planning, flight requests, and approvals
- Manage operations boards, calendars, and logbooks
- Maintain system and component records with maintenance tickets
- Run compliance workflows with evidence tracking and safety reviews
- Organise personnel, qualifications, assignments, and crew shifts
- Manage training calendars, attendance, and course progress

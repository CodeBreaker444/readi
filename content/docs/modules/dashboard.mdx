The Dashboard gives managers and pilots a real-time snapshot of the operation.

## Main capabilities

- Active mission count and status summary
- Fleet health — systems `OPERATIONAL` vs `MAINTENANCE` vs `NOT_OPERATIONAL`
- Upcoming planned missions for the day/week
- Recent alerts and notifications
- KPI widgets (safety, compliance, training completion rates)

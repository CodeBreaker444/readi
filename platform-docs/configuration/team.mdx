title: "Team Management"
description: "User accounts, roles, and access control."
---

Team Management controls who can access Readi and what they can do.

## Main capabilities

- Create and manage user accounts (name, email, role, timezone)
- Role assignment from a fixed set: PIC, OPM, SM, AM, CMM, MM, TM, DC, SLA, ADMIN
- Viewer and Manager flags for additional permission layers
- Territorial unit assignment for scoped access
- Resend activation invites to pending users

## User activation flow

New users receive an activation email with a one-time link. Until they activate, they appear as **Pending** in the team list. Use "Resend Invite" if the email was missed.

## Primary app routes

- `/settings/team`

---

Compliance keeps the organisation audit-ready at all times.

## Main capabilities

- Internal, external, and surveillance audit records
- Individual findings with severity and corrective action tracking
- Regulatory compliance requirements with status tracking
- Evidence document upload to support compliance claims
- Status change history log for each requirement

## Primary app routes

- `/compliance`
- `/compliance/audits`
- `/compliance/requirements`

Safety Management covers the full incident-to-action lifecycle and ongoing performance monitoring.

## Main capabilities

- File safety reports: incidents, hazards, observations
- Assign severity levels and corrective actions
- Track action status from open to completed
- Monitor KPI/SPI values against targets and thresholds
- View trend history across safety metrics

## Primary app routes

- `/safety`
- `/safety/reports`
- `/safety/actions`
- `/safety/kpi`

title: "Training"
description: "Course definitions, attendance tracking, and certification management."
---

## Main capabilities

- Define training courses with duration, type, and active status
- Record attendance: present, absent, excused
- Track completion status and scores (0–100)
- Issue certifications with expiry dates
- View training history per user

## Primary app routes

- `/training`
- `/training/courses`
- `/training/attendance`

---
title: "Document Repository"
description: "Centralised storage for operational documents, evidence, and regulatory files."
---

The Document Repository stores all operational documents in one searchable place.

## Main capabilities

- Upload and tag operational documents (SOPs, manuals, certificates)
- Attach documents to compliance requirements as evidence
- Filter by tags, document type, and upload date
- Non-operational drone warning — documents linked to a non-operational system are visually flagged

## Primary app routes

- `/records/documents`

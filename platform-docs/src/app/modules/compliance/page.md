Compliance tracks plans, evidence, review cycles, and schedule readiness.

## Main capabilities

- General audit plan lifecycle.
- Safety target review and approvals.
- Requirements and evidence management.
- Compliance calendar planning.

## Primary app routes

- `/compliance/general-audit-plan`
- `/compliance/safety-target-review`
- `/compliance/requirements-evidences`
- `/compliance/calendar`

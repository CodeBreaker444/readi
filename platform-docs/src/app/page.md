Welcome to the official documentation for Readi, an operations and compliance platform for drone teams.

Platform Overview{% .lead %}

Readi helps aviation and drone operations teams manage planning, missions, training, compliance, maintenance, and records in one workspace. It is built to support day-to-day operations while keeping audit readiness and safety visibility across teams.

## What you can do in Readi

- Track mission planning, flight requests, and approvals.
- Manage operations boards, calendars, and logbooks.
- Maintain system/component records and maintenance tickets.
- Run compliance workflows with evidence tracking and safety reviews.
- Organize personnel, qualifications, assignments, and crew shifts.
- Manage training calendars, attendance, and course progress.

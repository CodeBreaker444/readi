## 🚀 What's New

### Mission Planning
- Create and manage mission templates
- Planning dashboard with status overview
- Evaluation workflows before flight execution

### Flight Operations
- Daily operations board
- Flight request management and approval
- Operational calendar view

### Logbooks
- Mission, flight, and battery log tracking
- Drone and equipment management

### Safety & Compliance
- Safety management system
- Emergency contact management
- Audit plans, safety reviews, and requirements

- API key management

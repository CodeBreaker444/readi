## What's New

### Flytrelay Integration & Standardized Flight Logs

We have introduced direct, live integration with the Flytrelay network to help you monitor your fleet in real time. 

When enabled, your control center will automatically capture active flights running on Flytrelay hardware. Once a drone lands and the flight concludes, the system instantly processes the route into a standardized, universally recognized log layout. This builds a highly detailed, step-by-step historical preview of the entire flight path directly inside the platform.

---

### Simple Control Panel Toggle

To keep things clean and customizable, this feature can be turned on or off completely depending on your organizational needs.

- **Granular Control** — Administrators can easily activate or deactivate Flytrelay tracking for individual operators or owners.
- **Clean Operations** — Turning this toggle off keeps your platform interface focused solely on manual logs if your team doesn't use Flytrelay hardware, ensuring zero unnecessary clutter.

---

### Expanded Flight Preview Sheets

A specialized preview panel has been added to the Flights layout. When selecting a Flytrelay-tracked flight, coordinators can open a clean, scannable data sheet detailing exact altitude, positioning, and route milestones step-by-step—no need to export files or use third-party software.

---

### Attaching Planned Missions to Advanced (PDRA) Operations

You can now seamlessly connect a pre-scheduled, planned mission directly to your advanced operations during the creation process.

- **What's New** — The advanced operation creation window now includes a simple dropdown menu, allowing you to pull a planned mission straight out of your Mission Planning Logbook.
- **Where to Find It** — This option is available in the flight setup wizard.
- **Workflow Impact** — Once selected, the planned mission link is permanently stored alongside the operational record, making it easy to track pre-flight evaluations, view it directly on your operational calendar exports, and cross-reference planning requirements during audits.

---

## Improvements & Fixes

### Smoother Previews for Incomplete Flights

We resolved an occasional issue where opening the preview panel on a freshly registered or abruptly aborted flight would cause the screen to lock up or freeze if the flight data was still syncing. Empty or incoming paths will now safely display `0 Waypoints` and update dynamically as information flows in.

### Permanent Maintenance Document History

Previously, if a file uploaded during a maintenance ticket was later moved out of active status or replaced, it could disappear from the historical view. We have restructured the archive link to guarantee that any document uploaded within a maintenance log remains permanently visible and accessible, giving you a completely intact audit trail even if older files are archived or replaced.
